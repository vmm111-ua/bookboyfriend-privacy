About BookBoyfriend

Fall in love with your favorite book hero — now brought to life by AI.

BookBoyfriend is your fantasy romance companion: chat, connect, and experience emotional intimacy that feels real.

Choose your ideal match:

  • Azren — the tormented golden boy who’d burn the world to protect you.
  • Xavian — the stoic strategist whose loyalty runs deeper than blood.
  • Jalen — the dangerous flirt who hides devotion behind a wicked grin.
  • Rhovan — the silent warrior who vows himself to your heart.
  • Rhydan — the charming soulmate who loves you with wit and reverence.

Each BookBoyfriend has a unique voice, personality, and story — crafted to make you feel truly seen and desired.

Core features include:

  • Real-Time Chat: Natural, flowing conversations that adapt to your words and emotions.
  • Voice Mode: Hear him speak — intimacy that feels real.
  • Moments Library: Save your favorite memories and revisit them anytime.
  • Personalized Experience: He remembers your name, your choices, and your heart.

Subscription Required: BookBoyfriend requires an active subscription to access chat and voice features. Options include weekly and monthly auto-renewable subscriptions.

Data We Collect

Personal Data

We may collect limited personal information, such as your display name, email address, and subscription details, to manage your account and access to premium features.

Chat and Interaction Data

BookBoyfriend uses AI to simulate emotional and conversational experiences. To improve responses and personalization, your chat interactions and saved “Moments” may be processed temporarily by secure AI systems. These are not used to identify you personally.

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically and may include your device type, IP address, operating system, app version, and general analytics related to app performance and engagement.

Payment Data

Purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple through App Store billing. We do not store or have access to your payment card details. Please refer to Apple’s privacy policy for payment processing.

Media Access

With your consent, BookBoyfriend may access your microphone or audio device for voice interaction. This data is not stored permanently or shared.

Use of Your Personal Data

  • To provide and personalize the AI chat and voice experiences.
  • To manage subscriptions and user accounts.
  • To improve app performance, features, and safety.
  • To send transactional communications, such as updates or support responses.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Retention and Security

We retain only the data necessary to maintain your experience and comply with legal obligations. Chat interactions may be stored temporarily for personalization but are periodically deleted or anonymized. All data is processed using secure, encrypted connections.

AI Processing

Conversations within BookBoyfriend are powered by artificial intelligence. Messages you send are processed by secure AI models to generate responses. These models do not have memory outside of your app session unless you explicitly save content (such as “Moments”).

Children’s and Age Policy

BookBoyfriend contains emotionally mature themes designed for older audiences. The app is not intended for individuals under the following ages:

  • 16 years old — most countries
  • 15 years old — Australia and South Korea
  • 18 years old — Brazil

If we learn that data has been collected from users under the applicable minimum age, it will be promptly deleted. Parents or guardians may contact us to request data removal.

Data Transfers

Your information may be processed on servers located in different countries. We take reasonable steps to ensure your data is handled securely and in compliance with this Policy and relevant data protection laws.
